**Version:** 1.0.0

**Status:** Development

**Built in Zimbabwe. Designed for Everywhere.**

---

# Purpose

The SmartAware Engineering Exchange is the official engineering continuity system for the SmartAware Platform.

It provides a lightweight, structured method of transferring engineering knowledge between:

- Engineering sessions
- Engineers
- AI assistants
- Development workstations
- Future SmartAware engineering tools

The Engineering Exchange does **not** replace the SmartAware Development Workspace.

The Development Workspace remains the engineering source of truth.

The Engineering Exchange provides a concise engineering summary that allows another engineer to continue work without needing access to previous conversations or engineering sessions.

---

# Engineering Principles

The Engineering Exchange follows the SmartAware Engineering Philosophy.

In particular:

- Document Before You Develop
- Single Source of Truth
- Platform Before Product
- Engineer Once. Reuse Forever.

Engineering continuity shall depend on documented engineering knowledge rather than individual memory.

---

# Scope

The Engineering Exchange contains engineering knowledge only.

It shall never contain:

- Customer data
- Device secrets
- API keys
- Firmware binaries
- Private configuration
- Credentials

---

# Relationship to SmartAware

The SmartAware Development Workspace remains the engineering source of truth.

The Engineering Exchange is the official engineering handover mechanism.

Future SmartAware engineering tools may automatically generate or update Engineering Exchange documents.
