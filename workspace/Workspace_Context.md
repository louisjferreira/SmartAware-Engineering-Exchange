# Workspace Overview

The SmartAware Development Workspace is the engineering source of truth for all SmartAware platform and product development.

The workspace is organised around reusable engineering principles.

Products inherit capabilities from the SmartAware Platform rather than implementing duplicate functionality.

The SmartAware Engineering Exchange provides engineering continuity between engineering sessions, engineers and engineering tools.

# Current Engineering Philosophy

SmartAware engineering follows the following principles.

- Local First
- Privacy First
- Customer Owned
- Reliable Before Feature Rich
- Platform Before Product
- Modular by Default
- Single Source of Truth
- Document Before You Develop
- Engineer Once. Reuse Forever.
